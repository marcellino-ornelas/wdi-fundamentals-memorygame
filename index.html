Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Plmanism, Shinkei-Suijaku, Pexeso or simple Paris, is a card game in which alll of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards
